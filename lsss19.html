Logic Synthesis Software School

June 20, 2019 · EPFL · Lausanne, Switzerland · co-organized with IWLS

About the event

The Logic Synthesis Software School (LSSS) is an informal meeting on software tools for logic synthesis and verification. The goal of the meeting is to bring together reseachers and tool developers in the fields of electronic design automation, logic synthesis and verification to foster research on open sciece infrastructure and tools for logic synthesis and verification. LSSS is a joint event of IWLS and will take place on the June 20 at EPFL in Switzerland.
