Heinz Riener

Researcher, DLR, Bremen, Germany

Heinz Riener is a researcher in the Institute of Space Systems at DLR, Bremen, Germany, in the group of Avionics Systems.

  • From 2011 to 2015 he worked at the University of Bremen, Germany, in the group of Reliable Embedded Systems.
His research interests are formal methods for design and verification of hardware and software systems.
